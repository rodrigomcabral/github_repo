About We Cheff

We Cheffes is a project created to promote cultural integration. It's based in Lisbon althought this entrepeneuorship has been developed in many other countries, but mostly withoyt the success required.

The initiative makes possible for people from all over the world, to share their passion for cooking, as well as it's foods, but utterly for the consumer to be able to taste wherever food they want, homemade and in your house.
